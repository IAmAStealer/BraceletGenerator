Bracelet Generator

Welcome on Bracelet Generator Web Site !

Bracelet Generator is a friendship bracelet pattern designer. It enables you to easily design your own patterns, add rows and strings. A large choice of colors is available for the strings. The pattern can also be horizontally symmetrized. With the two-colored motif editor, you can create your motif and the pattern will automatically be generated. The motif can be rotated and symmetrized. The patterns can be exported in .png, .jpeg and .ps.

Development: Github repository, SourceForge page.
